MS DHONI

Captian Cool of INDIA

MS Dhoni is the only cricketer to win all the three ICC tournaments as captain. MS Dhoni led India to victory in the inaugural World T20 in his first-ever tournament as captain in 2007. He then guided India to their first World Cup title in nearly 28 years in 2011 and won the Champions Trophy in 2013.
